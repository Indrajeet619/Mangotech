Bees play important role in developing biodiversity. They help in pollination and also help in farming. But Bees honey can get affected by temperature and humidity. Bee farmers don’t have time to manually check bee hives, so we have developed iot device

IOT hardware

 		IOT device monitors temperature, humidity and Co2 levels in bee hives and farmer can get all info about hives on website. Also Co2 levels can help in detecting unexpected fires in hive or air quality in hive. We have used IBM code engine, mongodb database, IBM weather api, esp32 microcontroller, dht11 temperature and humidity sensor and CO2 sensor
